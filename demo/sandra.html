About Sandra:

Basic Training: We believe that the only workout worth doing is the one you look forward to. We take to the outdoors to find out inner strength. We help you build strength and definition without walls, mirrors or fifty different machines. Our full body resistance workouts are designed to help you build lean muscle in every area of your body. We run. We jump. We skip. We run some more. And we do it from the open vistas of Crissy Field to the hills of the Presidio and the dreaded sand stairs of Baker Beach. Log the miles with us and you'll come out faster, leaner and maybe even a little inspired. You haven't seen San Francisco until you've seen it from the trail.
