About Annie:

I focus on helping people get off the yo-yo diet treadmill. My tools help “diet suffers” to start feeling good about themselves from the inside out, and to achieve life-long, sustainable health & happiness.

Given my corporate background, my understanding of the corporate life also helps me to be an effective partner for corporations who want to raise the health & happiness index of their workforce. Healthy & happy employees = more productive employees.
