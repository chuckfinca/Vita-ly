About us

A perfect match

Specific is better than generic

The fitness and wellness professionals you’ll find in Vita-ly start with an assessment… someone that actually listens to you. Because programs and approaches based on your unique challenges and specific goals will beat a YouTube video, fitness app or chain weight loss center every time.

This is true generally. It’s especially true for Baby Boomers and others dealing with accumulated injuries or years of inactivity, or the many other challenges that come with advancing age or illness.
